About WrapRec

WrapRec is an open-source project written in C#. Feel free to contact us if you are interested to extend this project.

WrapRec is free of use. It aim to help researchers who work on Recommender Systems by providing a tool that facilitate evaluation of experiments.


How to Cite

Please cite the following paper in your article if you are using WrapRec.

WrapRec: An Easy Extension of Recommender System Libraries, In Proceedings of the 8th ACM Conference on Recommender Systems, RecSys '14, 2014


Acknowledgment

WrapRec is developed in the course of EU Project CrowRec, in Delft University of Technology.

The CrowdRec project investigates stream-based, context-aware Recommender Systems in social environments, building symbiosis with the crowd. Follow CrowdRec on Twitter.
